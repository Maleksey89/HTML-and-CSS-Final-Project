Weekly Workout Schedule

 Full Body 3x a week 1.5 hours Per Workout
Client: Johnny Doe

Monday

  1. Squats
    • Start Light, Just the bar as a warmup for 15 reps, Then add one 45lb Plate per side as another warm up set, these sets don't count toward set total
    • Super Set with Overhead Triceps Raises to maximize time usage at the gym
    • Total Working sets: 4, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 2-3, 3 min between sets
  2. Bench Press
    • Start light, One 45 lb plate per side for 2 warm up sets for 15 reps. These sets do not count towards total
    • Super Set with Bicep Curls
    • Total Working sets: 4, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 2-3, 3 min between sets
  3. Bent Over Rows
    • Start light, One 45 lb plate per side for 2 warm up sets for 15 reps. These sets do not count towards total
    • Super Set with Lateral Raises
    • Total Working sets: 4, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 2-3, 3 min between sets
  4. Barbell Shoulder Press
    • Start light, One 45 lb plate per side for 2 warm up sets for 15 reps. These sets do not count towards total
    • Super Set with Preacher Curls
    • Total Working sets: 4, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 2-3, 3 min between sets
  5. Deadlift
    • Start light, One 45 lb plate per side for 2 warm up sets for 15 reps. These sets do not count towards total
    • Super Set with Triceps PullDown
    • Total Working sets: 4, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 2-3, 3 min between sets
  6. Sleep and plenty of Protien, 1 gram per LB of bodyweight per day

Tuesday

  • Cardio Day
    • Either Zone 2 Cardio (Light Jogging) for 25 Min
    • OR LISS (brisk walk) for 1 hour, Can be spread in 2 1/2 hour sections
    • Sleep and plenty of Protien, 1 gram per LB of bodyweight per day

Wednesday

  1. Leg Press
    • Start Light, Just the bar as a warmup for 15 reps, Then add one 45lb Plate per side as another warm up set, these sets don't count toward set total
    • Super Set with Overhead Triceps Raises to maximize time usage at the gym
    • Total Working sets: 4, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 2-3, 3 min between sets
  2. Incline Bench Press
    • Start light, One 45 lb plate per side for 2 warm up sets for 15 reps. These sets do not count towards total
    • Super Set with Bicep Curls
    • Total Working sets: 4, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 2-3, 3 min between sets
  3. Pull Ups
    • As many as you can do per set, Attach weights if additional resistance is needed
    • Super Set with Lateral Raises
    • Total Working sets: 5, 3 min between sets
  4. Dumbbell Shoulder Press
    • Start light, 45 lb per hand for 2 warm up sets for 15 reps. These sets do not count towards total
    • Super Set with Preacher Curls
    • Total Working sets: 4, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 2-3, 3 min between sets
  5. Rack Pulls
    • Start light, One 45 lb plate per side for 2 warm up sets for 15 reps. These sets do not count towards total
    • Super Set with Triceps PullDown
    • Total Working sets: 4, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 2-3, 3 min between sets
  6. Sleep and plenty of Protien, 1 gram per LB of bodyweight per day

Thursday

  • Cardio Day
    • Either Zone 2 Cardio (Light Jogging) for 25 Min
    • OR LISS (brisk walk) for 1 hour, Can be spread in 2 1/2 hour sections
    • Sleep and plenty of Protien, 1 gram per LB of bodyweight per day

Friday-- Heavy Day

  1. Squats

    • Total Working sets: 3, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 1-2, 5 min between sets
  2. Bench Press

  3. Bent Over Rows

    • Super Set with Lateral Raises
    • Total Working sets: 4, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 1-2, 5 min between sets
  4. Barbell or Dumbell Shoulder Press
    • Start light, One 45 lb plate (Or 45lb weights) per side for 2 warm up sets for 15 reps. These sets do not count towards total
    • Super Set with Preacher Curls
    • Total Working sets: 3, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 1-2, 3 min between sets
  5. Deadlift
    • Start light, One 45 lb plate per side for 2 warm up sets for 15 reps. These sets do not count towards total
    • Super Set with Triceps PullDown
    • Total Working sets: 3, With a RIR (Reps in Reserve) of 1-2, 5 min between sets
  6. Sleep and plenty of Protien, 1 gram per LB of bodyweight per day

Saturday

  • Cardio Day
    • Either Zone 2 Cardio (Light Jogging) for 25 Min
    • OR LISS (brisk walk) for 1 hour, Can be spread in 2 1/2 hour sections
    • Sleep and plenty of Protien, 1 gram per LB of bodyweight per day

Sunday

  • Glorious Rest Day
  • Try for at least 1/2 hour of any cardio
  • At least be semi active during the day, active rest, this is not an excuse top be a couch potato today
  • As Usual: Sleep and plenty of Protien, 1 gram per LB of bodyweight per day
